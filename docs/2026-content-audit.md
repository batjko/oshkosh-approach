## Open discrepancy

The FAA Notice says to text `OSHARRIVAL` to `64600`; the current
[EAA Tips page](https://www.eaa.org/airventure/eaa-fly-in-flying-to-oshkosh/tips-for-flying-in)
lists `1-855-485-5674`. The app displays neither number until EAA confirms the
operative service. Do not resolve this by assumption.
